Interview @ Barclays

My interview experience with Barclays

Context

Hi, I'm Satyam Vyas, a MERN stack developer with a keen interest in Python. Currently, I'm working as an SDE Intern at SixLadders. I enjoy tackling challenges and finding optimal solutions. Lately, I've been expanding my expertise across the diverse domains of computer science.

Round 1: Technical Interview

  • Introduce Yourself
  • What was your first programming language? Why do you still use it, if you do?
  • Why not C++ or Java? Why Python?
  • You mentioned OAuth in your project; how does it work? Explain the system design of OAuth briefly.
  • OAuth: Does it handle authorization or authentication?
  • Explain JWT (JSON Web Tokens) and its sections.
  • How do we attach or send the JWT with each request?
  • Explain the different types of tokens that exist.
  • Tell me about the tools you've mentioned (Jupyter, Postman, Swagger, Figma).
  • What are microservices?
  • Name any microservices you've worked with.
  • What is your development interest: Frontend or Backend?
  • What is an API?
  • You mentioned Competitive Programming (CP) on your profile. What do you do in CP?
  • Design problem: Create a system with 3 elevators in a building. How would you manage them for optimal operation?
  • Follow-up: If two lifts are at the ground floor, the third one is going from the 6th to the 10th floor, and someone at the 8th floor calls the lift, how do you improve the design?
  • Have you worked with Java?
  • Can you name the four pillars of OOP in Java?
  • What is Polymorphism?
  • Give real-world examples of Inheritance.
  • You mentioned AWS and GCloud on your resume. Can you name some services they provide?
  • Why is cloud computing so popular?
  • Have you hosted any projects before? Explain the process and flow.
  • What is the difference between CE (Computer Engineering) and CSE (Computer Science Engineering)? What specialization would you choose if CE offers many options?
  • Any questions for me?

Round 2: HR + Technical Interview

  • Introduce Yourself
  • Tell me something not on your resume.
  • Share your technical achievements from before you started college.
  • What is MERN? Why choose JavaScript frameworks specifically?
  • Have you tried any other frameworks?
  • Can you name some web development frameworks in different languages?
  • Are you familiar with Java?
  • What is an abstract class?
  • Describe a situation where you identified a problem with yourself and how you resolved it.
  • What problem did you solve yesterday?
  • Describe a problem that took a week to resolve.
  • Tell me about a time when you helped someone.
  • You mentioned architecting an LMS system on your resume. Can you elaborate on that?
  • Why use MERN over other tech stacks?
  • If placed on a team working with a framework you're unfamiliar with, what would be your approach?
  • How would you react if locked in a room with someone of a different religion?
  • Would you be open to marrying someone from a different religion?
  • What is your general workflow for full-stack projects?
  • What best practices have you learned, especially concerning backend development?
  • Mention the Barclays values (RISES) and provide one example where you applied those in your projects.
  • What is scalability? How do you make a website scalable?
  • What is a load balancer? How would you implement it?
  • How do we test a website and determine the load it can handle?
  • Mention a few task scheduling algorithms. What does Windows utilize?
  • Do you think we should allow people with a grade < 6 to sit for placements? If they haven't done their studies properly, how will they excel at the job?
  • Any questions for me?
